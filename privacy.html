("Company," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy governs your access to and use of https://aguywithplans.com (the "Site") and describes how we collect, process, store, and protect your information when you interact with our website, request a B2B workflow audit, or engage our custom software and automation services. ### 2. Information We Collect We collect information that identifies, relates to, or could reasonably be linked with you ("Personal Information"): * **Voluntarily Provided Information:** When you submit an inquiry or claim a workflow audit via our web forms, we collect your name, email address, business name, and any details or project descriptions you voluntarily provide. * **Automated System Data:** When you visit our Site, standard web server logs automatically capture basic technical metadata, including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, and timestamp. ### 3. How We Process and Use Your Information We use your information strictly for legitimate corporate and operational business purposes, including: * Processing inbound audit requests and client inquiries. * Routing real-time auto-responder confirmations and technical follow-ups. * Analyzing operational requirements to design custom workflow automation systems. * Maintaining corporate records and compliance ledgers. **We do not sell, rent, lease, or trade your personal information to third parties or marketing brokers under any circumstances.** ### 4. Data Architecture & Third-Party Service Providers To deliver instantaneous, sub-second execution speeds without maintaining unnecessary database vulnerabilities, our Site utilizes secure, enterprise-grade third-party infrastructure providers: * **Form Intake Engine (Tally.so):** Web form modal overlays are processed via Tally.so using SSL/TLS encryption. * **Workflow Automation Engine (Make.com):** Submitted form payloads trigger secure webhooks on Make.com for real-time routing and automated execution. Data processed via Make.com is handled in-memory and passed directly to designated destinations. * **Customer Data & Storage (Google Workspace):** Inbound inquiries are logged securely into our Google Sheets CRM and processed via Google Workspace APIs (OAuth 2.0). * **Web Hosting Infrastructure (GitHub Pages):** Our static codebase is hosted securely via GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.). Each third-party service provider operates under its own privacy policy and security standards compliant with industry standards. ### 5.
